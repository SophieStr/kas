Recipe

Makes up to 12 delicious jaffas
Ingredients:
  • 80g Caster Sugar
  • 180g Butter
  • 240g Plain Flour
  • 1tbsp Milk
  • 1tsp Baking Powder
  • 0.5tsp Vanilla Extract
Instructions:
  1. Preheat the oven to 190C.
  2. Add the caster sugar, flour, baking powder and butter into a large bowl and rub together with your fingers until you create a breadcrumb texture.
  3. Add the 1tbsp of milk and 0.5tsp of vanilla extract.
  4. Knead into a firm dough.
  5. Roll out to aprox 3-4mm thickness. Remember flour your rolling pin too so it doesnt stick to your dough.
  6. Using your favourite shaped cutter (about 7 or 8cm in diameter), circular is traditional. Cut out as many biscuits as possible from the dough.
  7. Place as many biscuits as you can on a baking sheet, dont worry they dont spread very much, if at all.
  8. Bake in the centre of the oven for 9 mins until light golden.
  9. Remove from oven and allow to cool, they will crips up a bit as they cool.
