About KAS

Join us as we rate some of Britain's favourite biscuits on the 3 T's criteria and of course the WOW factor.

Texture - Whether crumbly, crunchy or gooey each biscuit will get a rating for its texture.

Taste - Some reckon this is the most important of the T's. This is where we score the biscuit on how it tickles the tastebuds.

Tea compatibility - A biscuit without a cuppa to accompany it is like a king without a castle, powerless. Here we'll let you know exactly how well the biscuit goes with a hot cup of tea.

WOW factor - How well will this biscuit impress at your birthday party, book club or town hall meeting. This score should help you out if you really want to WOW people people with your impeccable taste in biscuits.
