Knightsbridge

This Knightsbridge apartment runs adjacent to Sloane Street and is characterised by its stately terraces and column-bound entranceways. The apartment is a thoughtful balance of bold textures, modern artwork and furnishings, and a softer side of contemporary architecture.

Belgravia

Penthouse Apartment, Belgravia is a fusion of heritage exteriors and contemporary interiors, creating an open, warm, and grandiose space. The rich colour palette, comforting details and furnishings formulate a modern and yet timeless design.

Family House,
Melbourne

“Family House” in Melbourne, Australia, is synonymous with its surroundings, but its design and interiors exude a blend of Australia’s open-air living with the all the comfort of a quintessentially British home. This new-build property is a house that purposefully serves as a home away from ‘home’.
